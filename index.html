Turning Separate Offers Into One Strong Sales Campaign

Combined room packages and F&B promotions into one live commerce campaign across Chatrium properties, making the offer clearer to communicate, promote, and sell across channels.

SITUATION

Room packages and F&B promotions across Chatrium properties were being promoted separately, which made the campaign harder to communicate clearly across sales touchpoints.

TASK

Bring the offers into one stronger campaign, improve execution efficiency, and drive measurable revenue growth through live commerce, paid media, and cross-property communication.

ACTION

Planned the promotion structure, coordinated campaign communication across properties, managed content flow, live rundown, paid media placement, back-end coordination, and cross-channel promotion.

RESULT

The campaign achieved +63% revenue growth, 7.8 ROAS, 277K+ Facebook Live views, and a 70% reduction in production cost through a more efficient in-house workflow.

Brand Launch Case Study

Building a Korean-Minimal Brand From Zero

Helped build kiki&co from zero as a Korean-inspired minimalist hair styling brand, covering positioning, competitor analysis, visual direction, marketplace SEO, storefront design, content style, and paid ads.

SITUATION

CBG’s main beauty gadget portfolio had a Japanese-inspired direction, while the company wanted to launch kiki&co as a separate Korean-inspired brand for hair styling tools. At the same time, the market was crowded with brands using overly feminine, cute, or princess-like aesthetics.

TASK

Create a distinct brand identity for kiki&co, define its positioning, shape the visual direction, and support the launch of a new hair styling product line that could stand apart from competitors and perform on marketplace channels.

ACTION

Defined the positioning, built the Korean-minimal direction, analyzed competitors, developed moodboards and benefit-led copy, briefed designers, directed Shopee/Lazada storefronts, and used keyword research to focus the launch on high-demand core hair styling products.

RESULT

Launched kiki&co as a distinct brand with a clearer Korean-minimal identity and a new hair styling product line. Despite early marketplace product-upload issues, sales gained traction once the full product range went live across Shopee, Lazada, and TikTok, reaching 7-figure THB annual sales with a 500K+ THB peak month.

Social Commerce Case Study

Building Demand Through the Right Social Proof

Built demand for premium beauty gadgets by selecting KOLs for conversion potential and using X reviews, TikTok content behavior, TikTok Shop opportunities, and marketplace signals to build trust without celebrity endorsement.

SITUATION

Premium beauty gadget products needed to build credibility and demand in a competitive market without relying on expensive celebrity endorsement or the usual Instagram-led beauty promotion approach.

TASK

Identify the right platforms, KOL segments, and social proof formats to make higher-priced beauty gadgets feel trustworthy, understandable, and worth buying across social commerce and marketplace channels.

ACTION

Selected KOLs by conversion potential, audience fit, content behavior, and credibility. Used X reviews for searchable social proof and TikTok/TikTok Shop signals to test demand on a younger platform.

RESULT

Strengthened credibility, purchase intent, and marketplace performance through platform-specific KOL selection, X reviews, TikTok-led content behavior, and social proof strategy. This contributed to #1 category performance for a key beauty gadget product without celebrity endorsement.
